Our Story

Built on Passion &
Precision

American Iron Auto Works was founded in 2008 by Mike Callahan, a lifelong car enthusiast who believed every vehicle deserves to look its absolute best. What started as a one-man operation in a rented garage has grown into a full-service body shop with 12 dedicated craftsmen.

We specialize in American muscle — Mustangs, Camaros, Challengers, classic Chevys — but we love every car that rolls through our doors. If it's got four wheels and needs work, we're the shop for it.
